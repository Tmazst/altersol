Chief Executive Officer / Town Clerk

Our Client, an Urban Local Government, is looking to fill this vacancy.

AlterSol Consulting Limited

The Position

The Chief Executive Officer/Town Clerk oversees all aspects of running the town, working with the staff and Council to enforce decisions and laws created by the Council. A dynamic, innovative and solution-driven individual, the incumbent shall be responsible for providing strategic leadership, ensuring effective implementation of the Council policies and procedures, managing the council’s operations and promoting socio-economic development in the town.

Key roles and responsibilities

  • Provide strategic management to the Council
  • Develop and implement Integrated Development Plans and Budgets
  • Provide information, technical advice, decision-making support and secretarial support to Council and Council Committees
  • Ensure implementation of Council resolution, programmes, policies, projects
  • Ensure proper town development through implementing and monitoring approved township development policies, Capital Investment Program, resolutions and budgets of the Town Council
  • Ensure good corporate governance practices in the Municipality
  • Properly represent the town to the media and public
  • Lead robust service delivery and address concerns of town residents

Requirements

  • Master’s Degree in: Public Administration, Business Administration/Leadership
  • 5–10 years experience in an executive management position in a medium to large organization
  • A qualification in economics, urban design & planning or similar qualification will be an added advantage
  • A good understanding of local government and/or public sector management
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills to convey ideas to staff and interact positively with stakeholders
  • Delegation skills to assign tasks to department heads
  • Knowledge of local laws and policies
  • Critical thinking skills to approach complex town issues creatively
  • Financial skills for effective budget management
  • Negotiation and problem-solving skills to handle difficult situations
  • Diplomacy when handling sensitive and possibly divisive matters

Application Proceedure & Deadline
