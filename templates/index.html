Who We Are


Having observed leadership and management gaps in the corporate and development space in Eswatini and the SADC Region, the need for alternative solutions became evident, resulting in the birth of ALTERSOL CONSULTING (Pty) Limited.

Altersol is a management and development consultancy and training company focusing on helping organizations on a wide range of business and development challenges. Our company offers services to private companies, NGOs, state-owned enterprises, international development agencies, government departments, and farmers organizations.
